# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give actionable, technical feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination
- Deliberate intimidation or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent

## Scope

This applies to all repository spaces, issue discussions, pull requests, and related project communication channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private issue or contact maintainers directly. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled fairly.
